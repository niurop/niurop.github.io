Thus, I will only use licenses that promote sharing and progress.

My views on the topic.

Ideas are fundamentally different from natural resources, thus should not be treated as ones.

  • Natural resources in contrast to ideas, are finite.
  • Thinking of something does not prohibit others from thinking of the same thing.
  • Taking something from someone is not equivalent to coping someones ideas.
    In prior the original owner loses something with no trivial way of getting it.
    but in later nothing is lost by the owner.

Even more importantly law should promote fairness and ensuing safety, not providing moneymaking facilities.
It is obvious that patents and licenses don't follow those premises, especially the last one.
