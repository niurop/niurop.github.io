Simple recepi book.

Motivation:

Have you ever found yourself in a situation when you dont know what to cook.
Me too.

Hence interacional simple recepi book.

Specification:

  • Database of properties and units (mass, volume, sweetness, etc.).
  • Database of all ingridients and they properties(mass, volume, etc.).
  • Database of recepies.
  • Unit calculator (including volume to mass using database).
  • Personal databases (can modify and add personal info).
  • Powerfull serch engine (using custom filters with automatic recalculations when needed).
