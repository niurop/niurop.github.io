BipBip app.

Motivation:

I often find myself wanting to signal something simple to someone but writing or phoning seems cumbersome.

Simply just send two bips as "ok" and long beep as "no".

Specification:

  • Minimalist.
  • Overlay for pre connected conversations.
  • Visual and audio notification.
  • Timestamps.
  • Posibility for sending preconfigured symbols.
  • App for pc and phone.
